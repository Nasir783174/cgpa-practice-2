CGPA Information

◎

What is CGPA?

Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is the overall measure of a student's academic performance across all semesters, calculated as a weighted average of grade points.

◉

How is it calculated?

CGPA = Σ(Grade Point × Credit Hours) ÷ Σ(Total Credit Hours). Each course's grade point is multiplied by its credit weight, then averaged across all semesters.

◈

Why does it matter?

A strong CGPA opens doors to scholarships, higher studies, and competitive jobs. Many universities and employers use CGPA as an initial screening benchmark.
